About Us

Leading Eye Care in Surrey

Weybridge Eye Clinic has been at the forefront of ophthalmic care since 1995, combining clinical excellence with genuine compassion for every patient.

Our clinic brings together a team of highly qualified consultant ophthalmologists, each specializing in different aspects of eye care. We pride ourselves on delivering personalized treatment plans using the most advanced technology available.

Fully regulated and regularly inspected to ensure the highest standards of care.
